Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Mud Boll ("the App") is an iOS application provided by Atsushi Oshima ("the Developer"). Please review this Privacy Policy before using the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect or transmit any personal information. All game data, including mud ball growth records and statistics, is stored solely on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

Push Notifications

The App offers push notifications to remind you of daily care tasks. You can configure notifications at your discretion and disable them at any time through your device settings. No information related to notifications is transmitted externally.

Third-Party Data Sharing

The App does not provide, sell, or share your information with any third parties.

Advertising

The App does not contain any advertising.

In-App Purchases

The App does not contain any in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

The App is available for use by anyone regardless of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
