Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

OneMinuteClean ("the App") is an iOS application provided by Atsushi Oshima ("the Developer"). Please review this Privacy Policy before using the App.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect or transmit your personal information by default. Game data such as cleaning completion records and streak information is stored only on your device.

However, if you choose to use the following optional features, data will be transmitted to our servers:

  • Cloud Backup: Your cleaning completion records are backed up to AWS servers.
  • Family Sharing: Cleaning completion records are shared with your group members. Only completion records are shared; no other personal information is included.

Authentication

If you use cloud features, authentication is handled through AWS Cognito. Your authentication credentials are securely managed and are not used for any purpose other than providing the App's services.

Push Notifications

The App offers push notifications to remind you of your daily cleaning tasks. You can configure notifications at your discretion and disable them at any time through your device settings. No information related to notifications is transmitted externally.

Third-Party Data Sharing

The App does not provide, sell, or share your information with any third parties.

Advertising

The App does not contain any advertising.

In-App Purchases

The App does not contain any in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

The App is available for use by anyone regardless of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
